Introduction
This primer is intended to outline the current practice,evidence, and guidelines for the management of patientsadmitted to the Cardiology B-teams for angina, myocardialinfarction, and related cardiac conditions. Most of the patientsadmitted to the B-teams present with either unstable angina ornon-ST-elevated myocardial infarction. As such, this book isdevoted mostly to discussing the pertinent evidence that hasyielded the current practice and guidelines for the managementof patients with UA/NSTEMI. In addition, this 2012 updateincludes focused summaries of the diagnosis and managementof other commonly encountered cardiovascular conditions. This primer reflects the latest thinking within the academiccardiology community. We urge you to be mindful that thisthinking is continually in flux; cardiology advances quickly, andnew trials are continually being performed and published.
As always, clinical judgment with regard to an individual patient’s care takes precedence over any set of recommendations or guidelines.
